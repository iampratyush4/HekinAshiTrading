# HekinAshiTrading
Heikin Ashi candlestick chart involves calculating each candle based on both the current and previous periodsâ€™ prices. Heikin Ashi candles are different from traditional candlesticks in that they use average price movements to help filter out market noise, making trends easier to spot. The calculations for each Heikin Ashi candle are as follows:

Close: The average of the open, close, high, and low prices.
Open: The average of the open and close of the previous candle.
High: The maximum of the high, open, or close of the current period.
Low: The minimum of the low, open, or close of the current period.
